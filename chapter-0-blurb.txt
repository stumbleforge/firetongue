In a world where magic lurks in the shadows, Jod finds his quiet life upended when his people are brutally attacked by the Empire's soldiers. Forced to flee with a mysterious woman named Kisa, he embarks on a journey to uncover the truth behind the massacre and the dark forces at play. Along the way, Jod learns the world is not as it seems, and the chain of events set in motion will pit him, his family, and his people against the most formidable threat the world has ever faced.
